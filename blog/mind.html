Research Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

Growth mindset rejects an ontological state of mindset that is assumed to be fixed and unchangeable. Understanding the growth mindset is relevant as it enables one to embrace failure, as well as ambiguity and uncertainty that might emerge throughout learning, knowing that these feelings don’t define who you are is important.

In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

at first this idea of growth mindset sounds quite cliche and I thought it is obvious, but as I explored it and reflected on it more, it occurs to me that sometimes I am trapped in some sorts of fixed mindset that I don’t realise.

How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

I would like to reflect on feelings of defeat, uncertainty and doubt, trying to not think about these feelings as they define me, but rather part of a process.
